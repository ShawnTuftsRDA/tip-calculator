Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I tip at a restaurant?
15-20% is standard for good service in the US. 25%+ for exceptional service. 10% minimum for poor service.
Do I tip on pre-tax or post-tax?
Most people tip on the pre-tax amount. Tipping on the total is slightly more generous and also acceptable.
How much to tip for food delivery?
15-20% is standard. At minimum $3-5 regardless of order size, more for large orders or bad weather.
Should I tip at counter service?
Counter service does not traditionally require a tip, but leaving $1-2 is appreciated for good service.
